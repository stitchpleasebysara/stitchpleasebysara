📋 Terms & Good to Know

No Returns

All items are custom-made, so all sales are final. If there's an issue with your order, reach out and I'll always make it right.

Color Variation

Thread colors may vary slightly. I'll do my best to match and will reach out if I'm unsure.

Design Complexity

All designs are limited to a 4×4 hoop on the Brother SE700. If your request is too complex, I'll reach out to simplify before starting.

Custom Order Approval

For $250+ custom orders, I'll share a mockup before stitching begins so we're both happy.

Donation Verification

I'll verify all confirmation numbers before starting any order. If it can't be verified, I'll reach out before proceeding.

Order Expiry

Orders not confirmed within 2 weeks may be cancelled. US shipping only.
