Terms & Good to Know

No Returns

All items are custom-made, so all sales are final. If there's an issue, reach out and I'll make it right.

Color Variation

Thread colors may vary slightly. I'll do my best to match and will reach out if unsure.

Design Complexity

All designs are limited to a 4×4 hoop. If your request is too complex, I'll reach out to simplify before starting.

Donation Verification

I verify all confirmation numbers before starting any order. US shipping only.
